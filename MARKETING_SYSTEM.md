# VASTRA Growth System

## Instagram Content Engine

### Weekly cadence

- 1 reel daily (7 reels/week)
- 1 carousel every 2 days (3 to 4 carousels/week)
- 5 to 8 stories daily (BTS, polls, restocks, social proof)

### Reel pillars

1. `3 outfits under ₹999`
2. `How to style basic clothes`
3. `Before vs After styling`
4. Fit checks and transitions
5. Customer unboxing clips

### Carousel pillars

- “How to build a capsule wardrobe”
- “Top 5 best sellers this week”
- “Fabric care and longevity tips”
- “Outfit formulas for college/office/weekend”

### Hook templates

- "Stop wearing basics like this."
- "This ₹999 outfit looks like ₹4999."
- "If your fit feels boring, do this."

## Influencer Strategy (2k–10k followers)

### Target profile

- Fashion creators with strong engagement (not just follower count)
- City mix: Mumbai, Bangalore, Delhi, Hyderabad, Pune
- Audience: 18–30, style/fashion/lifestyle focused

### Offer model

- Free product seeding
- Optional affiliate code (10% with trackable commission)
- 1 reel + 2 stories minimum deliverables

### Outreach message template

Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] from VASTRA.

We love your styling content and would love to send you a curated VASTRA fit for a collaboration.
If you like the product, we'd like to plan 1 reel + 2 story frames featuring your look.

Happy to share moodboard and product options.
Would you be open to collab this week?

## Conversion events to track

- Product page view
- Add to cart
- Initiate checkout
- Coupon applied
- Purchase complete

## CRM / Retention basics

- Welcome email: brand story + first-order coupon
- Post-purchase: styling recommendations + review request
- Win-back email at day 21 for inactive customers
