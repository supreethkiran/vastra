# VASTRA Brand Identity

## Brand Positioning

VASTRA is a premium Indian fashion brand built around minimal silhouettes, elevated basics, and everyday confidence.

## Core Style

- Minimal
- Modern
- Luxury
- Street-smart

## Color System

- Black: `#0B0B0B`
- Off-white: `#F5F5F5`
- Accent Gold: `#C2A878`

Recommended usage:

- **Primary backgrounds:** Black
- **Primary text on dark:** Off-white
- **Accent actions:** Gold

## Typography

- Display: `Bebas Neue` (headlines, brand impact)
- Body/UI: `DM Sans` (readability, product info)
- Editorial accent: `Cormorant Garamond` (premium tone)

## Logo System

Files:

- `assets/branding/vastra-logo-dark.svg`
- `assets/branding/vastra-logo-light.svg`

Composition:

- Text logo: `VASTRA` in uppercase with letter spacing
- Icon: geometric `V` monogram in framed square

## Voice & Messaging

- Confident, concise, premium
- Fashion-forward but accessible
- Focus words: crafted, elevated, intentional, timeless
